Pricing FAQ

What does "setup" cover?

Full configuration of every product in your bundle — connecting your tools, training the AI on your business, testing, and going live. We do everything.

What's included in the monthly support hours?

Adjustments, updates, new integrations, troubleshooting, and improvements to your automation stack. Unused hours don't roll over.

Can I start with Foundation and upgrade later?

Yes. You pay the difference in setup fees and move to the higher monthly rate. We migrate your configuration — nothing is lost.

What happens after the commitment period?

Everything moves to month-to-month at the same rate. Cancel with 30 days notice.

Do I need technical knowledge to use these products?

Not at all. We handle all implementation and you interact with simple dashboards and reports. No technical skills required.

Can I add individual products to a bundle?

Yes — any product from a higher tier can be added à la carte at individual pricing. Or upgrade your whole bundle to the next tier.
